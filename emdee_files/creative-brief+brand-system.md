Website development

Engineered.

4. Homepage Structure
1. Navigation

Background: Deep Navy
Logo: White
Menu text: White
Primary CTA button: Electric Cyan

CTA Button Example:
"Get Started"
"Book a Consultation"

Button must:

Have subtle glow

Slight hover scale (1.03–1.05)

Smooth transition (200ms ease)

2. Hero Section

Background: Deep Navy
Headline: White
Subheadline: Light gray
CTA: Electric Cyan

Layout:
Left side text
Right side clean tech illustration or subtle gradient mesh

Headline tone:
Clear and strong.

Example style:
"Reliable Digital Infrastructure for Growing Businesses"

Avoid vague marketing language.

3. Services Section

Background: White

Each service card:

White background

Soft shadow

Navy title

Gray description

Cyan icon accent

Card spacing should feel premium and breathable.

Services to include:

Website Development

Managed Hosting

Maintenance & Support

Automation Systems

4. Why Makesoft Section

Background: Light Gray

3–4 credibility blocks:

Performance

Security

Scalability

Long-term partnership

Use simple iconography (line icons, not cartoon style).

5. CTA Section

Background: Deep Navy
Headline: White
Button: Electric Cyan

Big centered layout:
"Ready to strengthen your online presence?"

Strong contrast.
No clutter.

6. Footer

Background: Deep Navy
Text: Light gray
Links: White
Hover: Electric Cyan

Clean and structured.
No overcrowding.

5. Typography

Style: Modern Sans-Serif

Recommended:

Inter

Geist

Satoshi

Hierarchy:

H1: Bold, strong, high contrast
H2: Semi-bold
Body: Regular weight
Small text: Slightly muted gray

No decorative fonts.
No playful typography.

6. Button System

Primary Button:

Background: Electric Cyan

Text: Deep Navy or White (test for best contrast)

Rounded corners: Medium (not pill-shaped)

Padding: Generous

Shadow: Subtle glow

Secondary Button:

Outline style

Border: Electric Cyan

Text: Electric Cyan

Transparent background

7. UI Interaction Rules

Smooth transitions (200–300ms)

Hover brightness increase on buttons

Subtle elevation on hover for cards

No excessive animation

No parallax overload

It should feel polished, not gimmicky.

8. Imagery Style

Use:

Clean tech illustrations

Abstract network visuals

Infrastructure/server imagery

Subtle mesh gradients

Avoid:

Overused stock business people

Cartoon graphics

Loud gradient overload

9. Conversion Strategy Built Into Design

Cyan = action
Navy = authority
White = clarity

Visitor flow:
Authority → Clarity → Proof → Action

Every section should gently guide toward booking or contacting.

10. Mobile Requirements

Maintain strong contrast

Full-width CTA buttons

Generous vertical spacing

No crowded text blocks

Keep hierarchy strong

Mobile must feel equally premium.

11. Technical Preferences (If Using Tailwind)

Accent:
cyan: 400 / 500

Neutrals:

Avoid default blue palette.
No purple variants.
